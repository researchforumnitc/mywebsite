About

Mission and Vision

Vision

Research Forum NITC is formed to bring together research scholars across various disciplines to promote social and organizational activities, representation, and academic support.

Mission

The forum will be responsible for building and maintaining a healthy academic culture and an atmosphere conducive to carrying out various activities for the welfare of research scholars. It will foster the exchange of ideas to boost innovation and meet industry needs across the globe. The forum also aims to promote career opportunities across disciplines and to raise awareness of corporate, academic and research prospects within India and abroad. Besides, it also aims to raise the concerns and demands of the scholars' community to the administration. n, and academic support.

Ideas

Motivating Interdisciplinary Research and Applying for Funding Opportunities:

  • Creating interdisciplinary research teams consisting of researchers from different fields
  • Encouraging interdisciplinary research teams to apply for funding opportunities
  • Organizing workshops or events that bring together researchers from different fields to discuss potential areas of collaboration and interdisciplinary research

Facilitating Collaborative Research:

  • Creating an awareness about ongoing research at NITC
  • Educating researchers about the research facilities such as equipment, machineries, and software
  • Inviting special lectures/workshops to educate researchers about hot trends in the industry
