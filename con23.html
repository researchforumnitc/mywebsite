About

The Research Forum of NIT Calicut is excited to bring to you the first edition of our Research Scholars Fest- Constellate '23!

Constellateâ€™23 is envisioned as an event that recognises the contribution of scholars to research, community and culture at NIT Calicut. The Research Forum of NITC is joining hands with the Centre for Women Welfare and Social Empowerment (CWSE), and the theme for this year's fest is 'Women in STEAM', recognising the need to make our places of learning inclusive.
We aspire to bring together scholars from all fields of knowledge to exchange experiences and learn, network and develop new skills. With participation from institutes across South India, Constellate will feature interactive workshops, open lectures, panel discussions, lab visits, research demonstrations, masterclasses and much more!
The Research Fest will have something for everyone, whether you are an academic, a student, a community stakeholder or an industry partner. We will hear from well-established research networks, faculty, and transdisciplinary research groups. With events spanning an entire day, we seek to offer the ideal combination of ideas and opportunities for researchers to achieve academic, professional, and personal success. We would love your presence here for Constellate'23 on March 8, 2023, at NIT Calicut.
